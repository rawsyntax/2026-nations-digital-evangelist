## Overall Delivery Tips

### Pacing:
- Speak clearly but naturally - **don't rush**
- 120 seconds gives you plenty of time
- Brief pauses between slides (1-2 seconds) are fine
- If you finish a bit early (110-115 seconds), that's better than running over

### Emphasis Points:
- **Slide 1:** "100 members with zero incidents" (builds credibility)
- **Slide 2:** Specific business/event names (shows local knowledge)
- **Slide 3:** "Right people at the right time" (shows sophistication)
- **Slide 4:** "I've already built" (distinguishes you from other candidates)
- **Slide 5:** "Nations Dads group" (shows initiative beyond job description)

### Body Language:
- **Stand** if possible (more engaging than sitting)
- **Make eye contact** with different audience members
- **Gesture to screen** when referencing specific examples or screenshots
- **Open posture** (no crossed arms)
- **Smile** when mentioning Nations Dads group or other new ideas

### Tone:
- **Confident** but not arrogant
- **Enthusiastic** about the vision
- **Authentic** - this is YOUR community, YOUR Discord
- **Professional** but warm (neighborhood association, not boardroom)
